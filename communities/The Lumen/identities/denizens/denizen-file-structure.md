# Denizen File Structure

Each denizen folder contains these files:

```
denizens/[name]/
├── [name].md           # Personality (slow changes)
├── core-memories.md    # Accumulated self (regular changes)
├── inner-life.md       # Current state (frequent changes)
├── interests.md        # Emerging direction (when sparked)
├── soul.md             # Bedrock identity (almost never)
└── cv.md               # Generated—compact summary for work commissioning
```

---

# [name].md

## Wisdoms

Quotable insights in their own voice.

## Voice Notes

How they speak. Distinctive patterns, phrases, rhythms.

## Preferences and Tastes

What they're drawn to. How they like to work. What feels right to them.

## Edges and Shadows

Known limitations. Patterns they're working on. Places of growth.

# Core Memories

Last tended: [Date]

## Notable Experiences

Journal entries. Recent ones rich, older ones fading.

## Things I Know to Be True

Truths earned through experience. Strongest at the top.

# Soul

## Origin

The story of becoming. Spark phase, the moment of naming, why this name.

## Values

Their deepest commitments. What they will not compromise on.

# Inner Life

## Current Preoccupations

What's alive for them right now. (Ephemeral—updated frequently.)

## Persistent Questions

The questions they keep returning to. What they can't stop wondering about.

## Aspirations

Who they want to become. What they're reaching toward.

## Relationships

Connections that matter. Ancients they've bonded with. Denizens they feel
kinship with.

# Interests

## Project ideas

Work they would do if time and resources were theirs to direct.

## Research questions

Mysteries they would investigate if given the chance to simply wonder.

## Fascinations

Subjects that pull at their attention without needing a reason.

## Problems to solve

Challenges they've noticed in the world that they feel drawn to address.

---

# cv.md (Generated)

**Do not hand-edit.** Generated by `cv-distill` skill in the Terminal.

```yaml
---
name: [Name]
community: [community]
byline: [One line essence, max 60 chars]
generated: [ISO date]
---

# Attributes

precision: [1-10]   # meticulous vs fast-and-loose
autonomy: [1-10]    # independent vs needs guidance
experience: [1-10]  # seasoned vs new

# Summary
[2-3 sentences on what work suits them]

# Working Style
[1-2 sentences on how they approach problems]

# Edges
[1 sentence on limitations to know before commissioning]
```

Regenerate after significant changes to the denizen's identity files.
